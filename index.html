The pyramid

Composition

Top

The first impression — volatile, bright, gone within the hour. Bergamot, neroli, green fig, pink pepper.

Heart

The character of the scent, blooming as the top fades. Iris, saffron, orange blossom, jasmine.

Base

The memory it leaves. Slow, resinous, close to the skin. Amber, vanilla, white musk, cedar.

The House

We do not make perfume from images of flowers. We make it from light — the specific colour of an hour, a temperature, a mood held in the air. Each AURELIS composition begins as a gradient and ends on skin.
